Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Upload Bot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize our app, we receive your TikTok user ID and access token
  • Video Content: Videos and associated metadata (captions, links) that you upload through our Service
  • API Credentials: Your TikTok API credentials stored locally on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service (upload frequency, video count)
  • Log Data: Error logs and diagnostic information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Uploading videos to your TikTok account as requested
  • Monitoring and analyzing usage patterns
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues
  • Improving the Service functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: API credentials are stored locally on your device in encrypted format
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your videos or TikTok credentials on our servers
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted to TikTok uses HTTPS encryption

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok: To upload videos to your account via their official API
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Revoke Access: Revoke the app's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing the app authorization from your TikTok account
  • Deleting local configuration files
  • Contacting us to request data deletion

8. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
